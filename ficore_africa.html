The Challenges in Gombe Business Landscape

In Nigeria, individuals and SMEs face challenges with financial management. In Gombe State, Civil Servants, Entrepreneurs, Artisans, Households, and SMEs lack simple, integrated, culturally relevant, and user-friendly tools that address their practical needs to help them attain profitability with comprehensive record-keeping, reliable bill management, and accessible, certified financial education. This leads to avoidable business challenges and missed economic opportunities. We address these challenges through targeted finance tools, and enhanced learning platforms.

Our Core Proposition

1. Ficore Records (with its native Tax Function)

Value: A powerful, multi-function financial record-keeping system tailored for both personal and SME transactions. Its standout feature is an integrated native taxation function, simplifying tax compliance and financial organization – a critical, but mostly overlooked need. Users can manage the money they owe, the money they are owed, each of the payments they made out, and each of the receipts that came in, with the full support of Hausa language as the primary language.

How it brings Revenue to Ficore: It directly addresses compliance and operational efficiency issues, avoiding fines, ensuring sales opportunities are not missed, making it a premium, indispensable tool for recurring revenue.

Core Features that Truly Matter:
  • Multi-Function Financial Record System: Manage debts, credits, payments, and receipts in a way and format that matters to you.
  • Inventory Management with Smart Alerts: Tracks products and stock levels with notifications for low-stock, to prevent missed sales.
  • PDF Report Generation (ReportLab-Powered): Automatically generates clean, printable PDF reports for inventory, debts, credits, and transactions, useful for business reviews, loans, or tax filing.
  • Receipt Upload with GridFS: Securely upload, view, download, or delete receipts. Each receipt has a unique code and is tied exclusively to the user, mimicking physical record safety.
  • Coin-Based Feedback System: Engages users with virtual coins, earned by submitting feedback, with transparent transaction logs.

2. Bill Planner (Reminders & Tracking)

What it does: Provides a local-friendly dashboard to remind users of upcoming bills (rent, electricity, etc.) and track their payment status.

Why it matters: Proactively prevents penalties, enhances financial discipline, and gives users greater control over their expenses.

How it brings Revenue to Ficore: Opportunities for premium features like automated payment integration, SMS alerts, and bill synchronization.

3. Learning Hub (Certifications Included)

What it does: Offers comprehensive financial education and life skills courses designed for local relevance.

Why it matters: Taps into the significant demand for skill acquisition and provides tangible proof of learning through certificate issuance upon course completion – a powerful motivator, especially for job-seeking youth.

How it brings Revenue to Ficore: Monetizable through paid courses, strategic partnerships for NGO/Companies-sponsored access, and premium tier subscriptions.

Our strategy is built on low-cost entry points and community-driven growth, ensuring long-term sustainability and reach.

Pricing vs. Average Micro-SME Income

Ficore's token system offers features at a fraction of SME income, making financial clarity accessible.

Our Token-Based Subscription Model

How it Works:

  • Users buy coins with cash transfers (e.g., ₦500 = 10 coins).
  • Services cost coins: Simple report (2 coins), Invoice export (3 coins), Data sync (5 coins), etc.

Payment Approach:

  • No card payment required; users pay to a displayed bank account or via POS agent.
  • Proof of payment (screenshot or narration) is uploaded, and tokens are credited. Automated APIs (InterSwitch or Monnify) can be integrated later for those with access.

Why it Works in Gombe:

  • Removes card anxiety, works for unbanked users through POS transfers.
  • Feels local and trustworthy, aligning with cash-based preferences and avoiding recurring charges.
  • Provides flexibility (pay-as-you-go) and simplicity (1 coin = X value is easier to grasp).
